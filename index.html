What We Do

Real help.
No fine print.

  • Free Honest Estimates

    We tell you what remediation actually costs before anyone else gets the chance to overcharge you.

  • CalVCB Navigation

    Most families never know California's victim compensation program covers up to $70,000. We make sure they do and help them access it.

  • Insurance Advocacy

    We sit at the table so families don't sign away their rights under pressure from adjusters and companies.

  • Accountability Reporting

    We document and expose predatory pricing, fraudulent government contracts, and the exploitation of vulnerable people, publicly.

  • Direct Displacement Support

    For families with nowhere to go, we connect people to housing resources and fight for the stability they deserve.

  • Public Education

    We educate the public on consumer protections, victim rights, and the systemic practices that exploit vulnerable people.
